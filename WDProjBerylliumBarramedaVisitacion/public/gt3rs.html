The 2024 Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a track-focused marvel powered by a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine, producing 518 horsepower and 346 lb-ft of torque. Weighing just 3,268 pounds, it features active aerodynamics, generating 1,895 pounds of downforce at 177 mph. The GT3 RS sprints 0-60 mph in 3.0 seconds.
